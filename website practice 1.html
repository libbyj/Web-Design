Cabernet Franc

About the Grape

This is a grape that is a Bordeaux variety and used to be used mainly as a blending grape. Now, especially in cooler climates, it grows well enough to be its own wine. Wines made from Cab Franc are generally spicy, with dark red fruits.

New world wines that are made from Cab Franc are found in:

  1. Virginia,
  2. cool climates such as New York's finger lakes,
  3. and several west coast appelations.


Breaux Cab Francs

At Breaux Vineyards, we make a traditional Cabernet Franc called Marquis de Lafayette. In years that we feel the fruit warrants it, we also make a Cabernet Franc Reserve, such as the notable 2007 vintage. The warm 2012 vintage has inspired us to add a third wine to our Cab Franc lineup, the Marquis de Lafayette Reserve.
